Food around TIU that you should try

Phuoc An Nguyen

Last update: May 23th 2022

1.Tobu Kasumigaseki Ramen

〒350-1103 Saitama, Kawagoe, Kasumigasekihigashi, 1 Chome−２−21

This is a small ramen restaurant that near the Kasumigaseki Station, which make it accessible for everyone. The restaurant has a wide variety choices of ramen and side dishes for you to choose. Also, if you are a student, you can have an additional topping or dish for free. Rice is also provide free for anyone that would like to use with the exceed both from ramen, a traditional style of eating in Japan.

This restaurnt is suitable for one who want to have a quick meal after a day of working. The cozy space will help you to feel calm and relax after a long day at work. Also, this is a great place to go with small group of friend as the restaurant only have the table with maximum up to 4 seats per table.

Recommendation: えび豚骨塩ラーメン

2.ガスト Restaurant and Cafe

〒350-1101 Saitama, Kawagoe, Matoba 2243-1

This is a family restaurant that near the Kasumigaseki Station, which take only 3 minutes from the station and 5 minutes from TIU. The restaurant has a wide variety choices of dish for you to choose with an affordable price. The restaurant open until 11:30pm so it would be an ideal choice if you want to have a late dinner. One of the special thing about this restaurant is that they have a robot to deliver food to your table, which is an interesting experience for people who love technology.

This restaurnt is suitable for you to go with family. There are wide space in the restaurant but the maximum seats per table is 4. This is also a great place to visit at lunch time. With the drink bar (will cost you about 200yen), you can have unlimited access to a wide variety of drink. Moreover, there is free Wifi in the restaurant so that it would be a great place to sit and work for a while if you are waiting for next class.

Recommendation: Beef burger, drink bar

3.魚べい

〒814-7 Matoba, Kawagoe, Saitama 350-11011

This is a well-known chain of conveyor belt sushi restaurants established in 1990 in Japan. The restaurant is famous for the wide variey of sushi and side dishes with an afforable price.

This restaurnt is quite far if you go from TIU. However, if you are living in R1 or R2 dorm, the distance is not too far for you to visit once or you can ride by bicycle if you go from TIU. The restaurant is awlways crowed so that you should come early since you may have to wait for a while before having a seat. Howver, this is totally worth to wait. You can order from the tablet and the dishes will be delivered automatically to your table via a conveyor system.

Recommendation: Salmon, ice cream, all kind of Sushi
